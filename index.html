My Story

I started programming when I was seventeen, along with pursuing my bachelor in Computer sciences. I learned languages such as Python, JavaScript, and HTML. I engaged in various projects at Florida State University, such as the creation and designing of Git Pic. This app is Etsy inspired photograph organizing app which focuses on organization and ease of use.

I am currently in my senior year of college and have launched two more apps in the IOS store. One app is called Athan time and QuesTime. Both of these are excellent in providing reminders. However, Athan time is specific to the prayer timings, which notifies the exact timing of prayers. And Questime focuses on creating meaningful reminders and arranging to-do lists.

Now, I am working on creating two more major apps that will be released within a month. I wish to complete my Masters in computer sciences and later pursue my doctorate. During this learning period, I am passionate about creating innovative ideas and building high techs. I wish to learn more, practice more, and give more!
